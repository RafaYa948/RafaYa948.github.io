Who I am

Rafaya
Islam Diba

"Building things that matter, at the intersection of strategy, communication, and purpose."

I'm a digital marketing strategist and multi-venture builder based in Kuala Lumpur, operating across ASEAN. My work sits at the intersection of brand strategy, values-driven education, and experience design — three things that look different on the surface but share the same underlying discipline: figuring out what actually matters and building toward it.

I co-founded TAEI Academy — The Advancement of Empathy and Integrity — an institution working to close the gap between who people are and how they actually live and operate. We're currently rebuilding the organisation for adult professionals in Malaysia, bringing a decade of institutional history into an entirely new strategic context. It's some of the most purposeful work I do.

As Head of Strategy & Marketing at Centered Edge, I'm building the brand and content infrastructure for an executive leadership advisory firm targeting ASEAN's C-suite. That means getting the positioning right before anything is built, and building systems that will still make sense in five years.

I founded and organise TEDxAsia Pacific U — a full licensed TED event for the Asia Pacific university community. I almost didn't do it a second time. Then the first event day happened, and I stood in a room full of genuinely curious, ambitious people who had chosen to spend their time thinking harder about the world. That made the answer obvious.

My foundation is Computer Science with a specialisation in Cybersecurity. That technical literacy shapes how I think — analytically, precisely, with an obsession for getting the signal right in a world full of noise. Six years of hands-on marketing work across 550+ clients refined the execution side of that thinking.
